About Mon Etoile

Mon Etoile was born from a passion for astronomy and a love for Yerevan's unique night sky. We believe that the stars that witnessed your special moments should be remembered forever.

Based in the heart of Yerevan, our team combines astronomical precision with artistic design to create personalized celestial artwork that captures the exact configuration of stars or moon phase from your most cherished moments.

Whether it's a wedding, anniversary, birthday, or any milestone worth remembering, we transform these moments into beautiful physical art that you can display in your home or office.
